Project Overview

Terian Park Estate is an affordable housing project located in the outskirts of Kitengela Town, with frontage to the Nairobi-Namanga Highway. Developed on 30 acres of land, the project offers a mix of 1, 2 & 3 bedroom apartments designed for comfort and modern living.

Terian Park Estate will be a mirror of improvement from our previous successful affordable housing project, Terian Homes, supporting the Affordable Housing Program under Kenya's Big 4 Agenda.

The development combines affordability with quality, offering residents a complete living experience with comprehensive amenities and excellent connectivity to Nairobi and beyond.
